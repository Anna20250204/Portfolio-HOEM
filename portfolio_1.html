April Calendar

Canva, Illustrator / W 10inch x H 17inch

This is an event calendar for April, and I chose a marathon theme to reflect the challenges university students face during this month. April marks the final stretch before summer break, filled with exams and deadlines, making it feel like a race to the finish line. With this theme, I aim to motivate and encourage students to push through their academic marathon and reach the finish strong.
