Interact with paper

Paper, Photoshop, Illustrator / W 18inch x H 10inch

This project was learning transformation of 2D to 3D. I was able to observe the process of changing between 2D and 3D as I fold papers. In observing systems of organization and space making, I build upon the first semester's focus on two dimensions.

Overview

This project not only helped me understand 3D, but also gave me knowledge about photography and lighting. I took pictures with colored lights and various compositions to capture the interesting 3D shapes of the folded paper and the movement of the fonts placed on it.
