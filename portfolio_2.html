Pancake Breakfast Poster

Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva W 11inch x H 17inch

I designed this poster for HOEM's annual Pancake Breakfast event with each floor's Community Assistant. My goal of this poster was to capture the warmth and coziness of a comforting breakfast, creating a welcoming atmosphere that invites residents to enjoy good food and great company.
