Typography Essential book

Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign / W 10inch x H 7.5inch

This book is a classification of existing Roman-type typefaces. While working on this project, I learned about the history of each of the nine types and further learned information and characteristics of the typefaces that were three examples of the classification.

Overview

This is some mock-up pages that are part of book. While designing this chapter, I thought about how I could help readers access the history/classification of Typography from a more accessible and exciting perspective. I made it while thinking about it.
